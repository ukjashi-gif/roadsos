// First Response Guide
WHAT TO DO
AT AN ACCIDENT

Follow these critical steps immediately after a road accident to maximise survival chances.

01
🔴 Ensure Safety First

Turn on hazard lights. If safe, move away from traffic. Do not move injured persons unless there's fire or immediate danger.

02
📞 Call 108 Immediately

Dial 108 for ambulance. Stay calm and give your exact location, number of injured, and nature of injuries. Keep the line open.

03
🩹 Provide Basic Aid

Stop severe bleeding with firm pressure. Do not remove embedded objects. Keep injured persons warm and still until help arrives.

04
👮 Alert Police — 100

Call police to secure the scene, manage traffic, and document the accident for legal and insurance purposes.

05
📸 Document the Scene

Once immediate help is arranged, photograph vehicle positions, damage, road conditions, and any road hazards for records.

06
🔧 Call Vehicle Rescue

For vehicle recovery and towing, use RoadSoS to locate the nearest rescue service. Do not abandon your vehicle on a highway.
